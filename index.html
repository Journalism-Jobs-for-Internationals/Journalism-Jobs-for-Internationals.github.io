Our Mission

At JJI, our mission is to facilitate the professional growth of international journalists by providing them with the resources, guidance, and connections necessary to thrive in the competitive U.S. media landscape. We believe in the power of diverse voices and perspectives in journalism and are committed to fostering an inclusive media environment.

What We Offer

Job Placement Services

JJI offers personalized job placement services tailored to the unique skills and experiences of international journalists. Our team works closely with top U.S. media outlets to identify and secure opportunities that align with our clients' career goals.

Professional Development

We provide a range of professional development programs, including workshops, webinars, and training sessions, designed to enhance the skills and knowledge of international journalists. Our programs cover various aspects of journalism, from digital media and investigative reporting to ethical practices and media law in the U.S.

Networking Opportunities

Through our extensive network of industry professionals, JJI facilitates connections between international journalists and key figures in the U.S. media industry. Our events and networking platforms enable journalists to build valuable relationships and gain insights into the U.S. media market.

Visa and Immigration Support

Navigating the U.S. visa and immigration process can be challenging. JJI offers guidance and support to help international journalists understand their options and successfully obtain the necessary visas to work in the United States.

Career Coaching

Our experienced career coaches provide one-on-one counseling to help journalists refine their career strategies, improve their resumes and portfolios, and prepare for job interviews. We are dedicated to helping our clients present their best selves to potential employers.

Why Choose JJI?

Expertise

With years of experience in the media industry and a deep understanding of the challenges faced by international journalists, JJI is uniquely positioned to provide effective support and guidance.

Success Stories

Our track record speaks for itself. We have helped numerous journalists from around the world find rewarding careers in the U.S. media industry, contributing to the diversity and richness of American journalism.

Commitment to Diversity

JJI is committed to promoting diversity in the media. We believe that diverse perspectives are essential to a free and fair press, and we work tirelessly to ensure that international journalists have the opportunity to contribute their voices to the U.S. media landscape.
